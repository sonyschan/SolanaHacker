What is AI MemeForge?

AI MemeForge is a community-curated AI meme platform built on Solana. Every day, AI generates three unique crypto memes from real-time news. The community votes for free — no gas fees, no token purchases required. One winning meme is minted as a Solana NFT each day, and one lucky voter claims ownership forever.

How It Works — Daily Cycle

AI MemeForge runs a fully automated 24-hour cycle. Read the full daily cycle guide for a detailed breakdown.

Time (UTC) Event Details
00:00Meme GenerationGrok (xAI) fetches trending crypto news; Gemini (Google) generates 3 memes in randomized art styles
00:30 – 23:50Community VotingFree voting open to all Solana wallets. Each vote earns 1–10 base tickets + streak bonus (up to +10) + $Memeya bonus
23:50Winner DeterminedHighest-voted meme wins. Rarity tier assigned by percentile algorithm across all historical memes
23:55Lottery Draw + USDC RewardsWeighted random draw selects NFT owner. Crossmint wallet distributes 10% + 7% + 4% of treasury USDC to winner and 2 lucky voters

Note: Users can toggle "Don't Participate" to accumulate tickets across multiple days. Switching back to "Participate" before a draw leverages the full accumulated total — a core strategic mechanic.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to participate?

Nothing. Voting is gasless, the lottery is free to enter, and NFT claiming has no platform fee. You only need a Solana wallet — supported options include Phantom, Solflare, Backpack, and 100+ other wallets via Privy embedded wallet authentication. You can also sign in with Google (no existing crypto wallet required). The only cost a winner may encounter is ~0.01 SOL in network gas to mint their winning NFT on-chain.

How do I win an NFT?

Vote on daily memes to earn lottery tickets. At 23:55 UTC, a weighted random draw (cumulative scan algorithm) selects one winner proportional to ticket holdings. Example: If you hold 50 tickets in a pool of 500 total, your win probability is 10%. You can toggle "Don't Participate" to accumulate tickets across multiple days without entering the draw — then switch back to "Participate" on a day with a meme you love, entering the draw with your full accumulated balance. After any draw you participate in, your tickets reset to zero. Full details in the how it works guide.

What is the $Memeya token?

$Memeya is an SPL utility token on Solana (contract: mPj8dgqLDciVX27vU5efHiodbQhsgK43gGhjQrBpump). Holding tokens boosts daily ticket earnings using a logarithmic formula: bonus = floor(log10(tokens)). The diminishing-returns curve means going from 1K to 10K tokens adds only +1 ticket — rewarding broad distribution over whale concentration. Available on PumpFun. See the full tokenomics breakdown with all 7 bonus tiers.

How does the daily reward system work?

At 23:55 UTC each day, Memeya's Crossmint-powered Solana wallet (address: 4BqywEbjMf4APFBw1spPFr11q21Uu5A1fHpCRM2zSbMP) automatically distributes USDC rewards as a percentage of the treasury: 10% to the meme winner (most-voted meme's lottery winner), 7% to a randomly selected lucky voter, and 4% to a second lucky voter (21% total). Rewards scale with treasury balance. Rewards are sent directly to winners' Solana wallets — no claiming step required. Transactions are verifiable on Solscan.
