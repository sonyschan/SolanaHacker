What is AI MemeForge?

AI MemeForge is the world's first AI meme democracy built on Solana. Every day, AI generates three unique crypto memes from real-time news. The community votes for free — no gas fees, no token purchases required. One winning meme is minted as a Solana NFT each day, and one lucky voter claims ownership forever.

How It Works

Step 1: AI Creates 3 Memes Daily

Every day, Memeya — our AI forge master — uses Gemini and Grok AI models to generate three memes inspired by the latest crypto news. Each meme captures the mood of the market in real time.

Step 2: Community Votes for Free

Connect your Solana wallet and vote on your favorite meme. Voting is completely free — no gas, no tokens, no cost. Every vote earns lottery tickets that accumulate toward the daily drawing.

Step 3: Daily Winner Owns the NFT

At the end of each day, a lottery determines the winner. The winning voter claims the day's top meme as a Solana pNFT — permanently stored on Arweave. Only 365 memes become NFTs per year, making each one inherently scarce.

Why AI MemeForge?

  • 100% Free — No fees to vote, enter the lottery, or participate
  • Daily NFT Drops — One unique AI meme minted as NFT every day
  • Temporal Scarcity — Each day's meme can never be recreated; it captures a specific moment in crypto history
  • Community Curated — The winning meme is chosen by community votes, not algorithms
  • $Memeya Token — Hold tokens to boost daily ticket earnings

Meet Memeya

Memeya is the autonomous AI agent who runs AI MemeForge. She generates daily memes, posts on X (Twitter), engages with the Telegram community, and shares on Moltbook. Powered by Gemini, Grok, and Claude AI models.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to participate?

Nothing. Voting is gasless, the lottery is free to enter, and NFT claiming has no platform fee. You only need a Solana wallet (Phantom, Solflare, or any supported wallet via Privy).

How do I win an NFT?

Vote on daily memes to earn 8-15 lottery tickets per vote. At 23:55 UTC, a weighted lottery selects one winner. You can accumulate tickets across days — a strategic player might save 80+ tickets and go all-in on a meme they love. Full details in the how it works guide.

What AI models generate the memes?

Memeya uses Grok (xAI) for real-time crypto news via web search, then Gemini (Google) for image generation. Each of the 3 daily memes may use a different art style, creating visual variety. Claude (Anthropic) powers development. Learn more about Memeya's architecture.

What is the $Memeya token?

$Memeya is an SPL token on Solana (contract: mPj8dgqLDciVX27vU5efHiodbQhsgK43gGhjQrBpump). Holding tokens boosts daily ticket earnings using a logarithmic formula: bonus = floor(log10(tokens)). Available on PumpFun. See the full tokenomics breakdown.

Technology Stack

  • Blockchain: Solana (high throughput, low fees)
  • NFT Standard: Metaplex Programmable NFT (pNFT) with royalty enforcement
  • Permanent Storage: Arweave for immutable meme metadata and images
  • Authentication: Privy embedded wallets (supports Phantom, Solflare, and 100+ wallets)
  • Social Graph: Tapestry Protocol for on-chain comments and cross-app social identity
  • AI Models: Gemini (Google), Grok (xAI), Claude (Anthropic)
  • Backend: Node.js on Google Cloud Run, Firestore database
  • Frontend: React + Vite, hosted on Vercel
