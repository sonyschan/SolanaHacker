What is AI MemeForge?

AI MemeForge is the world's first AI meme democracy built on Solana. Every day, AI generates three unique crypto memes from real-time news. The community votes for free — no gas fees, no token purchases required. One winning meme is minted as a Solana NFT each day, and one lucky voter claims ownership forever.

How It Works

Step 1: AI Creates 3 Memes Daily

Every day, Memeya — our AI forge master — uses Gemini and Grok AI models to generate three memes inspired by the latest crypto news. Each meme captures the mood of the market in real time.

Step 2: Community Votes for Free

Connect your Solana wallet and vote on your favorite meme. Voting is completely free — no gas, no tokens, no cost. Every vote earns lottery tickets that accumulate toward the daily drawing.

Step 3: Daily Winner Owns the NFT

At the end of each day, a lottery determines the winner. The winning voter claims the day's top meme as a Solana pNFT — permanently stored on Arweave. Only 365 memes become NFTs per year, making each one inherently scarce.

Why AI MemeForge?

  • 100% Free — No fees to vote, enter the lottery, or participate
  • Daily NFT Drops — One unique AI meme minted as NFT every day
  • Temporal Scarcity — Each day's meme can never be recreated; it captures a specific moment in crypto history
  • Community Curated — The winning meme is chosen by community votes, not algorithms
